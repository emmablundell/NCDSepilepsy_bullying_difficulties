### Data availability

This study uses data from the [1958 National Child Development Study](https://cls.ucl.ac.uk/cls-studies/1958-national-child-development-study/) - a UK longitudinal cohort study that has followed over 17,000 people born in a single week of 1958, measuring social and economic factors, health and development. The data is available to researchers on application to the [Centre for Longitudinal Studies](https://cls.ucl.ac.uk/).
